Ayumu Uehara is a main character in Love Live! Nijigasaki High School Idol Club. A second-year student at Nijigasaki High School, she's a hard worker who takes anything and everything on diligently. She started being a school idol at the suggestion of her childhood friend. She's a highly caring friend who always supports her beloved pal in her endeavors.
